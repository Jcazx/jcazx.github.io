Bio

Daniel Caeser also known more personally as Ashton Dumar Norwill Simmonds is a singer songwriter born on April 5th of 1995 in Toronto Canada and is currently 25. He got his rise to fame after his highly rated praise break EP in 2014 and solidified his place in the music industry with his second EP Pilgrims Paradise and has garnered widespread acclaim from multiple sources. He spends most of his time creating soul,neo soul and R&B music. The Gospel and soul influence in his music is because of him growing up within the church. Since his rise to fame he has collaborated with artists like Rex Orange County, Chance the Rapper, Kali Uchis and Brandy. His most popular song is best part in collaboration with another R&B adjacent which allowed him to get a grammy for best collaboration in 2018. He has also been on two world tours 1 for each of his studio albums in 2017,18 for freudian and 2019 for case study 01
